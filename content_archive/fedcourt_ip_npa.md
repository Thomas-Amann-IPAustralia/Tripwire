# Intellectual Property [NPA logo with link NPA logo with link](https://www.fedcourt.gov.au/law-and-practice/national-practice-areas/ip)

## About this NPA

---

The Intellectual Property National Practice Area (NPA) covers disputes concerning matters such as patents, trade marks, copyright, designs, circuit layouts, plant breeder's rights and appeals from the Commissioner of Patents or Registrar of Trade Marks.

The Intellectual Property NPA consists of three Sub-areas:

* [Patents and Associated Statutes](https://www.fedcourt.gov.au/law-and-practice/national-practice-areas/ip/patents)
* [Trade Marks](https://www.fedcourt.gov.au/law-and-practice/national-practice-areas/ip/trade-marks)
* [Copyright & Industrial Design](https://www.fedcourt.gov.au/law-and-practice/national-practice-areas/ip/copyright)

[More on this NPA](https://www.fedcourt.gov.au/law-and-practice/national-practice-areas/ip/more#About)
