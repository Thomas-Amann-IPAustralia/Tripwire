National Health and Climate Strategy – Final thematic stakeholder engagement report
Publication type:
Report
Published:
Description:
The Australian Government has committed to making climate change a national health priority, by developing Australia’s first National Health and Climate Strategy.
To guide the development of this strategy, we released a consultation paper and hosted stakeholder engagement activities, including:
- face-to-face and virtual workshops
- ministerial round tables
- written submissions
- surveys.
