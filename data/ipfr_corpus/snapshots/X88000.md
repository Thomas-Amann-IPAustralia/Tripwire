The inaugural One Health Symposium was held in Canberra last week.
The event brought together more than 100 delegates from across the human, animal, plant and environmental health sectors to explore the future of disease prevention, biosecurity and integrated health systems.
Catch some of the highlights from the event in the video on our LinkedIn page.
The 2025 symposium was the first event of its kind hosted by the interim Australian CDC in partnership with the Australian Government’s One Health Network and is part of its broader mission to strengthen One Health approaches nationwide.
