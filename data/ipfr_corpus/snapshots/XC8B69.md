Collection description
The SoNG collection contains nationally consistent advice and guidance to help Public Health Units deal with notifiable diseases using best practice.
The guidelines have been developed in consultation with the CDNA and endorsed by the Australian Health Protection Committee (AHPC). They capture the knowledge of experienced professionals, built on past research efforts.
