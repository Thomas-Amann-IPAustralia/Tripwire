SSBA – Form – Non-registered facility report for suspected SSBAs and confirmatory testing results
Publication type:
Form
Published:
Description:
Non-registered SSBA facilities should use this form to:
- transfer a suspected SSBA for confirmatory testing
- destroy a suspected SSBA prior to confirmatory testing
- confirm the presence of an SSBA by doing your own confirmatory testing on a suspected SSBA
- report the results of a test on a suspected SSBA that was sent to another facility for confirmatory testing.
