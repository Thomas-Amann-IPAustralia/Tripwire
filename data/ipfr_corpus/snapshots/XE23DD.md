CDNA Guidelines – Information for healthcare workers living with a blood borne virus who perform exposure prone procedures
Publication type:
Fact sheet
Published:
Description:
This CDNA fact sheet gives information to health care workers who are living with a blood borne virus and perform exposure prone procedures.
It should be read with the Australian National Guidelines for the Management of Healthcare Workers Living with Blood Borne Viruses and Healthcare Workers who Perform Exposure Prone Procedures at Risk of Exposure to Blood Borne Viruses.
