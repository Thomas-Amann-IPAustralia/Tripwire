CDNA National Guidelines – Healthcare workers who live with blood borne viruses, perform exposure prone procedures, or are at risk of exposure to BBVs
Publication type:
Guideline
Published:
Description:
These guidelines contain the current expert consensus on the evidence for health care workers and their blood borne virus status.
The recommendations include measures to prevent transmission, and to manage and treat health care workers with:
- hepatitis B virus
- hepatitis C virus
- human immunodeficiency virus.
