This upcoming webinar will inform vaccination providers about respiratory viruses as the annual flu season approaches. It will include general information, information for high-risk individuals, and vaccine advice for:
- influenza
- COVID-19
- respiratory syncytial virus (RSV).
The webinar will be presented by the Head of the interim Australian Centre for Disease Control (CDC), Professor Paul Kelly and other subject matter experts.
