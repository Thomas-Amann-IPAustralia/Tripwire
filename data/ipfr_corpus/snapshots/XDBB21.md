Description:
This Communicable Diseases Network Australia (CDNA) fact sheet gives information to health care students who perform exposure prone procedures.
It should be read with the Australian National Guidelines for the Management of Healthcare Workers Living with Blood Borne Viruses and Healthcare Workers who Perform Exposure Prone Procedures at Risk of Exposure to Blood Borne Viruses.
