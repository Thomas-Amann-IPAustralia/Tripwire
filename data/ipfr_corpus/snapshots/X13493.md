le ActionThe NNDSS coordinates the national surveillance of diseases on the National Notifiable Disease List. These diseases are those that present a risk to public health if there is an outbreak.
This helps us identify trends in diseases, assess the impact of disease control programs and develop policies to reduce the impact of these diseases in Australia.
You can use this tool to explore the data and view interactive graphs and tables for these diseases across different variables, including:
- notification rates
- number of notified cases
- by state and territory
- by year
- by month
- by date range
- by age group
- by sex.
We update this data daily, with information provided by the states and territories.
For more information about how to use the tool, see our user guide.
