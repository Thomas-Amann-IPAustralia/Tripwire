enHealth guidance – The health effects of environmental noise
Publication type:
Report
Published:
Description:
This review looked at more than 200 research papers, publications and policies to assess the impacts of environmental noise. It makes 4 recommendations:
- recognise that environmental noise is a health risk
- promote measures to reduce environmental noise and associated health impacts
- address environmental noise in planning and development activities
- foster research to support policymaking and action.
