The Australian Government is establishing a CDC to improve Australia's response and preparedness for public health emergencies.
We released the Role and functions of an Australian Centre for Disease Control’ consultation paper on 10 November 2022, and invited select stakeholders to provide a written response against the paper. The CDC stakeholder consultation report summarises feedback from written submissions and workshop participation.
Please note the report is provided for information only and does not necessarily reflect our views nor that of the Australian Government.
