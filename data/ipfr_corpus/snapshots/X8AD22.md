About us
Our role in protecting Australia from public health threats and improving the health of all Australians.
Communicable disease prevention and control
We protect Australians against disease outbreaks and reduce the impact of disease on people and our economy.
Disease surveillance
Our surveillance and reporting activities, which help us identify the trends, impact and severity of communicable diseases.
Environmental health
We work with partners to manage environmental health in Australia, including air quality, toxins and occupational dusts.
Climate and health
We work across government to make the Australian health system and communities resilient and responsive to a changing climate.
One Health
We take a One Health approach in all our work, which recognises that human, animal and environmental health are all connected – the health of one affects all.
