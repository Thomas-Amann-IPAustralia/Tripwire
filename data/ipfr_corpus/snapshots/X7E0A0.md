Publication type:
Guideline
Published:
Description:
This guidance note covers known Australian tick-borne diseases:
- spotted fever group Rickettsia transmitted by ticks
- Queensland tick typhus (QTT)
- Flinders Island spotted fever (FISF)
- Australian spotted fever (ASF)
- Q fever.
