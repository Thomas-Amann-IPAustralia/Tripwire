You will need the following information to fill out this form:
The information required for an entity’s initial registration includes:
- entity and facility details
- Responsible and Deputy Responsible Officer details
- SSBA details, including your purpose for handling
- SSBA Standards compliance declaration.
You must submit this as a hard copy, by registered post or courier. See our contact for address details.
