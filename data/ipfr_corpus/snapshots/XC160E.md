Guidance note for medical practitioners and hospitals – Tick-induced allergies and tick-associated toxicosis and paralysis
Publication type:
Guideline
Published:
Description:
This guidance note covers the evidence on the currently recognised severe tick-induced allergies: tick anaphylaxis and mammalian meat allergy/anaphylaxis, and on mild allergic reactions from tick bites. It also covers the evidence on tick-associated toxicosis and paralysis.
