NORDR – Patient privacy and consent statement
Publication type:
Form
Published:
Description:
This document contains the National Occupational Respiratory Disease Registry patient privacy and consent statement. It sets out the step-by-step process for a physician to understand when patient consent is and is not required to notify information to the registry.
The document also contains:
- the patient privacy statement
- patient consent forms for the patient’s physician to pass to the patient to obtain their written consent to their information going into the registry.
