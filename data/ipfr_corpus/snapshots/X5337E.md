PHLN guidance regarding Mycobacterium chimaera and heater–cooler units
Publication type:
Guideline
Published:
Description:
This document helps laboratories with their approach to preventing and controlling heater–cooler unit-related infections.
It is based on the protocol published by Public Health England and is supplemented with laboratory experiences documented in the associated PHLN survey of Australian laboratories handling specimens.
