This webinar included short presentations followed by a panel discussion with the following speakers:
- Dr Joanne Walker: Capacity Building Program Manager, Wildlife Health Australia
- Niraj Meisuria: PhD Student, Disease Ecology Lab, University of Sydney
- Tanja Strive: Senior Principal Research Scientist, Health and Biosecurity, CSIRO
Speakers discussed:
- feral animals as a One Health issue
- wild animal carcasses and disease transmission
- One Health and rabbit biocontrol in Australia.
About the One Health Wonders webinar series
The One Health Wonders webinar series is a collaboration between the Australian Centre for Disease Control and CSIRO.
It showcases the relationship between human, animal and ecosystem health and promotes collaboration between academic researchers, state and federal government departments and One Health stakeholders.
