National Immunisation Strategy for Australia 2025 to 2030
Publication type:
Strategy or framework
Published:
Description:
The new National Immunisation Strategy for Australia 2025 to 2030 sets a vision for a healthier Australia through immunisation.
It is supported by a mission to reduce the impact of vaccine-preventable diseases through high uptake of safe, effective and equitable immunisation across the lifespan.
