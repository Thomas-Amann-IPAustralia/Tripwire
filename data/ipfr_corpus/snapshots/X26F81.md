Information Publication Scheme Agency Plan 2026 to 2028
Publication type:
Policy
Published:
Description:
The Freedom of Information Act 1982 requires all agencies to publish a range of information on their website under the Information Publication Scheme (IPS).
The IPS aims to increase transparency and encourage a culture of disclosure.
Our IPS Agency Plan describes:
- what information we will publish
- how we will publish this information
- how we will meet the requirements of the IPS.
