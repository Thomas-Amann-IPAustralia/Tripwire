CDNA Guidelines – Information for healthcare workers who perform exposure prone procedures who are NOT living with a blood borne virus
Publication type:
Fact sheet
Published:
Description:
This CDNA fact sheet gives information to health care workers who perform exposure prone procedures, and are not living with a blood borne virus.
It should be read with the Australian National Guidelines for the Management of Healthcare Workers Living with Blood Borne Viruses and Healthcare Workers who Perform Exposure Prone Procedures at Risk of Exposure to Blood Borne Viruses.
