By following these steps and providing the necessary information, you can efficiently submit an intellectual property (IP) issue on SHEIN.
1. Visit the IP Notice Section:
• Scroll to the bottom of the SHEIN homepage and click on IP NOTICE.
2. Access the Complaint Portal:
• In the initial section of the INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY NOTICE page, find and click the complaint portal link: www.shein.com/ip-complaint .
3. Review Required Materials:
• Read through the list of materials needed to file your complaint, then click BEGIN.
4. Specify Your Relationship to the Rights Holder:
• Select the relationship between the complaint submitter and the rights holder, then click CONFIRM.
5. Select Patent Type and Infringement Scenario:
• Choose the type of patent involved in your complaint. Additional options for specific infringement scenarios will appear. Select the appropriate scenario and click NEXT.
6. Provide Detailed Information:
• Submit comprehensive details including but not limited to the following, based on the type of IP issue:
Trademark Issue:
• URL or SKU number(s) for related product(s)
• Trademark or description of the trademark
• Trademark application or registration number (Optional)
• Date that you first used the trademark
• Links or files showing the use of the trademark
Copyright Issue:
• Description of the copyright
• URL or SKU number(s) for related product(s)
• Copyright Registration Number (if applicable)
• Date of first publication (date the work was first made available to the public)
• Links or files showing the copyrighted work
• Links or files showing the creation of the original work
Utility Patent Issue:
• Description of the utility patent
• URL or SKU number(s) for related product(s)
• Patent Registration Number
• Country of Patent Registration
• Links or files showing the registration
• Court order or judgment supporting your claim
Design Rights Issue:
• URL or SKU number(s) for related product(s)
• Design Registration Number (if applicable)
• Country of Design Registration
• Links or files showing the registration (for registered designs)
• Links or files showing the creation of the original design (for unregistered designs)
Click NEXT after providing the required information.
7. Enter Contact Information:
• Input your contact details, including but not limited to: the name of the rights holder, the agent's name, contact email, and mailing address. Click SUBMIT.
8. Sign and Confirm:
• A signature box will appear. Enter your signature and check the box to confirm that the information submitted is true. Click CONFIRM. You will see a submission success page, and wait for a response from the SHEIN team via email.
